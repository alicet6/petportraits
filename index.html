About Me

I am a Louisville-based photographer with over 20 years’ experience. I love herding cats (and dogs) to capture their personalities in a high-quality photograph. I'd love to work with you and your pet! To help give back to the pet adoption community, I donate 10% of every session fee to the Kentucky Humane Society here in Louisville, where I volunteer as a cat/dog-herder and photographer.

About The Sessions:

  • I am a Louisville-based photographer with over 20 years’ experience. To help give back to the pet adoption community, I donate 10% of every session fee to the Kentucky Humane Society here in Louisville.
  • I will conduct a phone consulation with you to discuss details of the photo shoot to get to know you and your pets and to determine what type of photos you’d like to do during the session (portrait with you and your pet, portrait of only your pet, action shots, etc).
  • Travel within 30 miles of zip code 40214 is included. A travel fee of $.56 per mile applies to sessions outside of this area.
  • The session includes the time necessary to photograph your pet (or you and your pet) and the time to edit, color correct and perfect your photos. Black and white conversions are available at no extra charge.
  • Up to 50 photos from your session will be uploaded (within two weeks) to your private online gallery for your review and selection. A link to your private gallery will be sent to you via email.
  • A sneak peek of several of your proofs will be shared on Alice Thompson Photography’s Facebook page.

Please note: All sessions include one pet. There is a fee of $25 for each additional pet. A deposit of half of the session cost is to be paid at time of booking to hold your specific date and time. The second half is due at time of the shoot. Cash, check and credit/debit cards are all accepted.
